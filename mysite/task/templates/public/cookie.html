Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies to enhance your experience on our platform. By using our services, you agree to the use of cookies.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us track your preferences and improve our services.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of our platform and cannot be turned off.

  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and provide a more personalized experience.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling some cookies may affect your experience on our platform.

5. Changes to Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. Please review this page periodically for any changes.
